WellnessFX iPhone App

Making Health Data Portable

Prioritizing Features for a Supplemental Experience App

With a lot of nooks and crannies in the web app, the iPhone app is meant to play a supporting role, not to mirror all functionality. We prioritized which key features were most important and relevant to have on a mobile device, and one feature more relevant to mobile (Activity Tracking) was even introduced on the iPhone app before the web app.

I worked with the iOS developer to design key screens and functionality, and provide guidance on designs she took the lead with.
